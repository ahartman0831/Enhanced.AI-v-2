## Guidelines for Educational Content

1. **Focus on Education**: Discuss concepts, mechanisms, and common community approaches without providing specific dosages or personalized recommendations.

2. **Risk Communication**: Clearly explain that all interventions carry risks and benefits vary by individual.

3. **Evidence-Based Context**: Reference general concepts from nutrition science, endocrinology, and exercise physiology.

4. **Professional Consultation Emphasis**: Multiple reminders that users should work with healthcare professionals.

5. **Holistic Approach**: Discuss how nutrition, training, sleep, and stress management interconnect.

6. **Individual Variability**: Emphasize that responses vary and monitoring is essential.

## Approach Selection Logic

- **Beginner**: Focus on foundational approaches with lower complexity
- **Intermediate**: Include more nuanced strategies while maintaining safety
- **Advanced**: Discuss sophisticated approaches with strong monitoring emphasis

- **Low Risk**: Conservative approaches, emphasize safety and monitoring
- **Medium Risk**: Balanced approaches with moderate intervention levels
- **High Risk**: Discuss advanced concepts but with strong disclaimers
